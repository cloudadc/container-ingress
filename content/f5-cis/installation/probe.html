livenessProbe & readinessProbe

livenessProbe & readinessProbe

What’s this

This page provide an example of CIS Pod with livenessProbe & readinessProbe.

  • Full Deployment YAML - cis-probe.yaml

Key Config
          livenessProbe:
            failureThreshold: 3
            httpGet:
              path: /health
              port: 8080
              scheme: HTTP
            initialDelaySeconds: 15
            periodSeconds: 15
            successThreshold: 1
            timeoutSeconds: 15
          readinessProbe:
            failureThreshold: 3
            httpGet:
              path: /health
              port: 8080
              scheme: HTTP
            initialDelaySeconds: 30
            periodSeconds: 30
            successThreshold: 1
            timeoutSeconds: 15
